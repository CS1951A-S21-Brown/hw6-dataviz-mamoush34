So the barplot graph by default shows the win percentages of the top 10 teams ranked in descending order, but the viewer has the chance to use next and previous buttons to see the next teams in order. However, it would be wise to bet on one of the top 5 teams listed in the initial 10. Note: Graph 2 is a map type, which is indicated as extra credit.
